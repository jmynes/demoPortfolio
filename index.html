VPC4

This is my most recent demo, and is for the Vocalist Producer Challenge 4, an annual event in the Nerdcore and Chiptune music communities. In the VPC, pairs of vocalists and producers team up and complete challenges for cash prizes and exposure. This site was built by me out of love for the community, voluntarily.

The VPC will start in a few weeks, and thus the front page has only placeholder texts (lorem ipsum) and example headers with partially related Soundcloud links to accompany them. There will be a rating system and login system implemented, and I'm currently trying to keep away from Wordpress for this particular design.
